# Script Framework

Write the strongest recommended topic as a 120-300 second Chinese short-video script. Use a口语化强观点 style: conversational, direct, vivid, plain-spoken, and evidence-backed.

## Structure

1. Three-second hook
   - Start with a strong judgment, result, number, or contradiction.
   - Avoid vague openings like "最近有个新闻".
   - Example: `AI 游戏开发真正恐怖的地方，不是它会画图，而是它开始生成整个世界了。`

2. Fact setup
   - Say who did what.
   - Mention why this source or demo matters.
   - Keep facts concise; do not bury the video in company names.

3. Value translation
   - Translate the news into what it means for AI game developers, solo creators, AI service sellers, or tool users.
   - Use "这件事真正重要的地方是..." to shift from news to insight.
   - After each abstract judgment, add a concrete scene that viewers can picture.

4. Mechanism breakdown
   - Explain how the tool/trend changes the workflow.
   - Use 2-4 numbered points.
   - Prefer concrete verbs: generate, test, prototype, package, sell, deliver, iterate.

5. Opportunity path
   - Explain what a practical creator can do next.
   - For monetization, avoid promising easy money. Frame it as service packaging, workflow improvement, or niche expertise.

6. Boundary and risk
   - State what the technology still cannot do.
   - This protects long-term trust and makes the viewpoint more credible.

7. Closing stance
   - End with a creator-positioning line.
   - Example: `以后真正值钱的，不是会不会用 AI，而是你能不能做出一个客户明天就能打开用、还能看见效果的 AI 工具。`

## Plain-Language Rules

- Use fewer abstract words and more visible scenes.
- When using terms like `落地`, `赋能`, `生态`, `交付`, `增长`, `效率`, `工作流`, `解决方案`, `商业化`, or `行业机会`, immediately explain them in plain Chinese.
- Every opportunity point should answer: who uses AI, in what scene, to do what, and what result they get.
- Every 3-5 sentences, name a concrete person or role such as `老板`, `员工`, `客服`, `销售`, `剪辑师`, `游戏工作室`, `电商店`, `培训机构`, or `独立开发者`.
- Avoid saying only `提升效率`; say what got faster, for whom, and by roughly how much when evidence supports it.
- Avoid saying only `可交付`; say the customer can open it tomorrow, use it in a real task, and see saved time, extra revenue, fewer mistakes, or clearer decisions.
- If a sentence sounds like a consulting slide, rewrite it as a real workplace scene.

## Voice Rules

- Use short oral sentences.
- Use "兄弟们" only when it fits naturally; do not overuse.
- Strong viewpoints are allowed, but every strong claim needs a fact, example, or boundary.
- Avoid exaggerated certainty such as "必爆", "稳赚", "完全替代".
- Keep technical terms but immediately translate them into plain language.
- Do not write like a press release.
- Do not stack professional nouns. Prefer one technical term plus one everyday example.
- Use strong viewpoints, but make them feel like spoken judgment, not industry-report prose.

## Output Pieces

For the recommended topic, provide:

- Title.
- Cover text, 6-14 Chinese characters if possible.
- 3-second hook.
- Full oral script.
- Suggested visuals.
- Comment prompt.
- Risk note.
